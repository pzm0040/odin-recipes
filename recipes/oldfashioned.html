Ingredients

  • 2 oz High Rye Bourbon
  • 1/4 oz Simple Syrup
  • 3 dashes Angosutra Bitters
  • 3 dashes Orange Bitters
  • Orange for Garnish

Steps

  1. Stir liquid ingredients in mixing glass. ~15 Seconds
  2. Strain into rocks glass.
  3. Garnish with orange twist.
