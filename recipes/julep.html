Ingredients

  • 8 Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 oz Simple Syrup
  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • Garnish: mint sprig
  • Bitters (optional)

Steps

  1. In a julep cup, lightly muddle mint leaves in simple syrup.
  2. Add bourbon and crushed ice.
  3. Stir drink.
  4. Garnish with mint sprig.
